    Production Pipeline

    Voice narration, background images, intro video, closed captions, and final video assembly.
    Voice Narration

    AI voice reads your story aloud. Choose provider and voice style per genre.

    OpenAI TTS ~$0.09 / story
    ElevenLabs ~$0.20 / story + plan
    Google TTS Free up to 1M chars/mo
    Phase 2
    Scene Image Generation

    AI generates the atmospheric background image displayed throughout your story video.

    DALL-E 3 $0.040 / image
    Stability AI $0.020 / image
    Ideogram $0.080 / image
    Phase 2
    5-Second Intro Video

    Animated opening clip from the scene image. Plays then freezes as story narration begins.

    LTX-2 API ~$0.30 / clip
    RunwayML Gen-3 ~$0.50 / clip
    Title Card (ffmpeg) Free
    Phase 3
    Closed Captions

    Auto-generated SRT captions synced to narration. Burned in or attached as separate file.

    OpenAI Whisper ~$0.006 / story
    TTS timestamps Free (from TTS output)
    Phase 4
    Background Music

    Genre-matched royalty-free music plays softly under the narration throughout the video.

    Included library Free (bundled)
    Epidemic Sound $15/mo subscription
    Phase 4
    Video Assembly

    ffmpeg combines intro clip + frozen frame + voice + music + captions into final MP4 for YouTube.

    ffmpeg (local) Free — runs on your machine
    Phase 4
    Total estimated cost per story (all phases, mid-range options): ~$0.50 – $0.90 per complete video Story generation ($0.046) + voice ($0.09) + scene image ($0.04) + intro clip ($0.30) + captions ($0.006) + music (free) + assembly (free) = ~$0.48 using recommended options.
